About

Born and raised in Cork city, I'm a software engineering student on the Immersive Software Engineering (MSc) programme at the University of Limerick, where the focus is as much on entrepreneurship and communication as it is on code.

I like working across the whole stack of computing — one week it's a Linux device driver in C, the next it's a Unity physics simulation, a React front-end, or routing a PCB for a rocket flight computer. I got my start teaching younger students to code at CoderDojo, and I still love breaking hard ideas down into simple ones.

Outside the terminal I'm a fluent Irish speaker with a real passion for language conservation — which is exactly why I'm building Ardán, a social platform for Gaeilge. I'm also a qualified sailing instructor, so I'm just as at home on the water as I am at a keyboard.
